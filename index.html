Our team

We formed our company in early September of 2021 and began researching potential problems we faced. Our goal was to find a potential problem and use that as the foundation of our business. We envisioned many things, however, we arrived at the common problem of cleaning your shoes and protecting your car.

The Problem

No matter where you live your shoes become dirty. No matter where you live you use transportation. No matter where you live your vehicle becomes dirty. Debris defender is the name you will hear when these things are said. We are here to help you prevent any mess from outside of your car from being tracked in. Snow, mud, and rain are just a few of the many messes tracked into vehicles every day.

What we're up to

As aspiring entrepreneurs, we understand the importance of innovation. After analyzing feasibility and effectiveness we have shifted our focus onto the brush itself. Now, as of February we are perfecting the design, creating an efficient portable, and reliable shoe cleaner.

Why Debris Defender

As debris defenders, we strongly believe in quality and convenience. With this new innovative idea, it is as simple as leaning over and brushing your shoes with our high-quality brushes that do the hard work for you. Within seconds you will be able to place the brush into your custom-provided pouch and drive off. Many of you ask what separates up from others such as Weather tech. We look to pair with weather tech as well as compete with them. Our design will prevent the mess before it even comes into your car, more inexpensive, and more efficient.
