Our team

We formed our company in early September and began researching potential problems we faced on a daily basis. Our goal was to find a potential problem and then use that as the foundation of our business. We envisioned many things, however, we arrived at the common problem of cleaning your shoes.

The Problem

Initially, we thought to create a shoe cleaning brush that would be attachable to the side of your car. We had to find a convenient location to place the cleaner, and then design a mechanism that would attach as well as the brush.

What we're up to

As aspiring entrepreneurs, we realized the importance of innovation. After analyzing feasibility and effectiveness we have shifted our focus onto the brush itself. Now, as of February we are perfecting the design, creating an efficient portable, and reliable shoe cleaner.
